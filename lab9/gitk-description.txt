Top Left Pane:
	Shows a series of commits to the repository, as well as the
	branches and any merges that have occurred.

Top Middle/Right Pane:
	These panes show the person and email of the person who performed
	the commit, as well as the date and time at which this event 
	occurred.

Bottom Left Pane:
	This shows the commit details, and you can select various views
	in order to see the full diff, the old version, or the new version.

Bottom Right Pane: 
	This pane shows the list of files impacted by the selected commit
